H1B Jobs Overview

The H-1B visa allows U.S. employers to hire foreign workers in specialty occupations. This visa is popular among professionals in IT, engineering, finance, and other specialized fields.

Services for H1B Job Seekers

  • Job Search Assistance: Connecting clients with U.S.-based employers seeking specialized skills for H-1B roles.
  • Resume Building: Ensuring resumes meet U.S. standards and highlight relevant skills.
  • Interview Preparation: Coaching for U.S. interview formats and employer expectations.
  • Employer Matching: Matching clients with companies willing to sponsor H-1B visas.
  • Job Market Insights: Providing information on in-demand fields and salary expectations.
